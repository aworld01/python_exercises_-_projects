How to install PyAudio in your computer.

  1. Open this link in your computer.
  2. Press CTRL + F key and type "pyaudio" and hit ENTER.
  3. Select your Python version and download
  4. Go to your download folder, click in address bar and write powershell.exe and hit ENTER.
  5. Type: pip install PyA and hit TAB for autocomplete and ENTER.

Congrates! your PyAudio has been successfully installed.
